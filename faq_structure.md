# Mailing Memories FAQ Structure & Content

## 1. Page Layout & Hierarchy
- **Header**: Simple title "Frequently Asked Questions" with a brief, calm sub-headline.
- **Navigation**: Sidebar or top-level category anchors for quick skimming (The Service, Shipping, Support).
- **Format**: Accordion style (Question/Answer) to keep the page clean and reduce cognitive load.
- **Tone**: Calm, clear, and professional.

## 2. Recommended FAQ Categories
- **The Service**: Details about the letter, materials, and pricing.
- **Shipping & Delivery**: Timeline and geographical coverage.
- **Help & Support**: Assistance with writing and order issues.

## 4. Design Notes
- **Typography**: Use the brand's serif font for questions and a clean sans-serif for answers.
- **White Space**: Generous margins to maintain a "calm" feel.
- **Visuals**: No salesy banners; perhaps a single, high-quality image of a clean envelope or quality cardstock.
