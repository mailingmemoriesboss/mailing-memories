FAQ

Keep this lightweight. Just enough to remove doubt before someone buys.

What’s inside the card?

A thoughtful, handwritten message in the tone you choose. You can add preferences, topics to avoid, and a sign-off style. (No “therapy” claims—just grounded encouragement.)

Can I pause weekly cards?

Yes. Weekly is meant to be easy. You can pause, skip weeks, or cancel anytime.

Is it always the same message?

No. Weekly cards rotate content with whatever is inspiring the writer at the moment. Always expect to get a fresh message, the fun is you never really know what to expect

Is this written by one person?

Cards are handwritten with consistent, neat handwriting. (We do not promise a specific writer.)
